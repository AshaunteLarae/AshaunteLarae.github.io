CISE

Welcome! We are CISE also known as PBP(ProtectBlackPeople). We're Black-Owened business based in LA and our motto for the Black Community is: 'Stronger Threads For a Stonger Community'. We've been up an running since 2010 and it all started when our team came together and decided to create an apparel line that gives a voice and a platform for Blaack people to express racial injustice & inequality.Our two most recent handbags lines are 'Protect Black People' & 'Protect Black Women'. In todays soceity it's extremely important that we keep raising awareness for our people and we plan on doing so by expanding on our brand.
