ABOUT US

Our mission will always be to support the Black community. We've been funnelig our proceeds into organizations begnning with the Anti-Racism Fund. As the company grows our goal is to also become a springboard for Black creatives. In Addition to the fact that we're online apparel store, we're also a also a full service creative workshop, communications, design and digital marketing agency. We hope that we're able to pull others into the frame of establishing brands that stand for something. By combining the efforts and proceeds from our store CISE, we'll be providng young Black entreprenuers who also want to use their artistic vision to raise awarenss while increasing the Black dollar.
